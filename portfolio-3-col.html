Advance.

“Advance Irrigation System” as the name suggest we tried to improvise the fertility of soil which is the core of a crop I.e., from where the crop gets it’s nurishment. We have read lot of research papers about the nourishment of a crop. We came to know that micro and macro nutrients plays a vital role in the development of a crop

Advance..

As we all know macro nutrients(the macronutrients: nitrogen (N), phosphorus  (P), potassium (K), calcium (Ca), sulfur (S), magnesium (Mg), carbon (C), oxygen (O), hydrogen (H)) are pretty much common which were been studied by many and tools were implemented too for their fertility but on the other hand micro nutrients(the micronutrients: iron (Fe), boron (B), chlorine (Cl), manganese (Mn), zinc (Zn), copper (Cu), molybdenum (Mo), nickel (Ni)) were ignored at some point and very few have build something to improve crop’s micro nutrient.

Advance...

as it seems , yes we worked on micro nutrients of a crop. As crop gets it’s nutrients from it’s soil so we checked the PH value of soil so that we can depict the micro nutrients which are present in the soil , and there have been many studies that says that we can depict the amout of micro nutrients and macro nutrient by measuring the PH of the soil.

every micro nutrient’s quality lies at certain pH level Therefore we have analyzed that at what pH the micro and macro nutrients are fully present. We have developed an application so that it’ll be much easier for the user to operate this whole miraculous system(Advance irrigation system or IP) at ease.
